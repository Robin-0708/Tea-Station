made in nilgiris

Nilgiri tea, cultivated in the picturesque Blue Mountains of southern India, is celebrated for its briskness, bright liquor, and balanced astringency. Handpicked and carefully processed, this tea showcases delicate floral notes, a subtle sweetness, and a refreshing finish. Whether in orthodox or CTC form, Nilgiri tea offers a versatile and delightful experience. The prized Nilgiri Frost tea, harvested during winter, stands out with its mellow, smooth taste and pronounced floral aroma. This tea, rich in antioxidants, is a favorite among connoisseurs for its unique terroir and exceptional flavor profile.

relaxation

Nilgiri tea, sourced from the tranquil hills of Southern India, brings relaxation to the forefront of its advantages. The tea's soothing and brisk flavor, coupled with its lower caffeine content, makes it an ideal choice for those seeking a calming and stress-relieving beverage. The serene aroma further enhances the relaxation experience, creating a moment of tranquility with every sip. As a delightful option for unwinding, Nilgiri tea stands out for its ability to promote a sense of ease and relaxation in the midst of a busy day, offering a comforting respite to rejuvenate the mind and body.

energy

It celebrated for its relaxation benefits, it also stands out for providing a subtle and sustained energy boost. The tea's moderate caffeine content contributes to a gentle stimulation, offering a natural and gradual pick-me-up without the jitteriness often associated with higher caffeine levels. Its invigorating properties, coupled with the tea's vibrant flavor profile, create a harmonious blend that not only relaxes but also revitalizes, making it a versatile beverage for those in need of a subtle energy lift. Nilgiri tea thus emerges as a refreshing choice, seamlessly balancing relaxation and revitalization in every cup.

receipe

The preparation of delightful cup of Nilgiri tea, start by boiling water to 190-200°F (88-93°C). Add 1 teaspoon of Nilgiri tea leaves per cup to a teapot or infuser. Pour the hot water over the leaves and let it steep for 3-4 minutes, allowing the flavors to infuse. Strain the leaves, and if desired, add a splash of milk or a sweetener to complement the tea's brisk and bright flavor. Nilgiri tea is known for its floral and fruity notes, so feel free to savor it without any additions for a pure and invigorating experience. Adjust the steeping time based on your preference, and enjoy the calming and refreshing essence of Nilgiri tea.
